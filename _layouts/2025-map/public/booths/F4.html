🚀 穿越时空的光影奇遇！✨

在这个充满创意与科技的小实验中，

孩子们将亲手制作一座闪耀的“时空隧道”，

体验电子电路与光影艺术的奇妙结合。💡

A Time-Travel Light Adventure! 🌠

Kids will build their own glowing “Time Tunnel”

and explore the magical fusion of electronics and light art

through this fun and creative experiment. ⚡

🎨 活动说明 | Activity Overview

1️⃣ 学习基础电子原理与灯光连接方式。

2️⃣ 使用 LED 灯、导线、电池与镜面纸，搭建发光隧道。

3️⃣ 观看灯光在镜面中形成无限延伸的炫酷效果！

1️⃣ Learn simple electronic concepts and light wiring.

2️⃣ Build the glowing tunnel using LEDs, wires, batteries, and reflective paper.

3️⃣ Enjoy the infinite visual illusion of lights stretching into the future! 🌈
