🎯 活动介绍 | Activity Overview

1️⃣ 选择案件 — 从四个案件中任选一个挑战。

2️⃣ 分组协作 — 六位侦探共同推理、分析线索。

3️⃣ 拼图找线索 — 观察、对比、拼合物证，揭开故事脉络。

4️⃣ 锁定嫌疑人 — 在限时内完成推理并写下最终结论。

1️⃣ Select a Case — Choose one of four mysterious cases to investigate.

2️⃣ Work as a Team — Collaborate in a group of six detectives.

3️⃣ Solve Through Puzzle Clues — Observe, compare, and piece together evidence.

4️⃣ Find the Culprit — Deduce the truth and record your final conclusion before time’s up. ⏱️
