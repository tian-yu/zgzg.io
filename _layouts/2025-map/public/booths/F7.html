🎨 让秋天的色彩，绽放在你的手帕上！🍂

用叶子、花瓣和大自然的颜料，

在布上敲打出独一无二的图案，

感受自然与艺术的奇妙融合。🌿✨

Create your own unique handkerchief using leaves, petals, and natural pigments.

Let the colors of autumn bloom on fabric and experience the perfect harmony of nature and art. 🌸🍁

🎨 活动说明 | Activity Overview

1️⃣ 选择喜欢的植物叶片或花朵，摆放在纯棉小方巾上。

2️⃣ 使用小槌轻轻敲打，让植物的天然色素渗入布料。

3️⃣ 展开后即可看到由自然印制的专属图案。

1️⃣ Pick your favorite leaves or petals and place them on a cotton handkerchief.

2️⃣ Gently hammer to transfer the natural pigments onto the fabric.

3️⃣ Unfold your cloth to reveal a one-of-a-kind nature print. 🌼

💡 小贴士 | Tip: 每一次敲打，都是与大自然的温柔对话。💚

💡 Tip: Each tap of the hammer captures nature’s beauty in its purest form. 🌿
