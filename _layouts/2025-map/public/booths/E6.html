一场猫与老鼠的欢乐竞速赛！🏁💨 每组玩家分成猫方与鼠方， 用扇子为气球“加油”吹风， 让它们沿着赛道滚向终点“老鼠洞”—— 看看究竟是机灵的小鼠先逃脱， 还是灵敏的猫咪先捕到猎物！🐭🎯

A fast and fun balloon chase between cats and mice!

🐱🌟 Players are divided into Cat and Mouse teams, using fans to “blow” their balloons along the track to the finish line—the Mouse Hole!

Who will be the first to escape, the quick mouse or the clever cat? 🎈

🎮 游戏规则 | Game Rules

1️⃣ 玩家分成猫鼠两队，各使用一支扇子。 2️⃣ 用扇子为气球扇风，让气球沿赛道向前滚动。 3️⃣ 气球先到达终点“老鼠洞”的一方获胜！

1️⃣ Players divide into Cat and Mouse teams, each with a fan. 2️⃣ Use the fan to blow air and propel the balloon along the track. 3️⃣ The team whose balloon reaches the "Mouse Hole" finish line first wins! 🏆

👫 适合人数 | Recommended Players: 4人及以上 / 4+ players

💡 小贴士 | Tip: 保持扇风的频率与方向，让气球稳稳向前冲！💡 Tip: Keep a steady pace and direction with your fan to keep the balloon on course!
