一场紧张又欢乐的反应与勇气游戏！🏃‍♂️💨 “守护者”挥动大球保护奶酪， “老鼠们”则要在不被打中的情况下冲进圈内， 抢到小球（奶酪）再安全带回自己的地盘！🧀🎯

A fun and exciting game of speed and strategy!

🐭💥 The Guardian swings the rope with a large ball to protect the cheese, while the Mice must rush in, grab the small balls, and escape safely— without getting hit!

🧀⚡

🎮 游戏规则 | Game Rules

1️⃣ “守护者”慢慢挥动绳子，让大球绕圈旋转，并可逐渐加快速度。 2️⃣ 其他玩家扮演“老鼠”，需躲避大球进入圈内捡起一个小球。 3️⃣ 每次只能拿一个小球，并迅速退回圈外放在自己身后。 4️⃣ 若被大球击中，需退出一轮或放回捡到的小球。 5️⃣ 当所有小球被捡完时，游戏结束！

1️⃣ The Guardian swings the rope in a circle, slowly increasing speed.

2️⃣ The Mice must dodge the ball and run into the circle to grab one small ball.

3️⃣ Each mouse can take only one ball per round, then return to safety.

4️⃣ If hit by the large ball, the player must sit out one round or return their ball.

5️⃣ The game ends when all the small balls (“cheese”) are collected!

👫 适合人数 | Recommended Players: 4人及以上 / 4+ players

⚡ 小贴士 | Tip: 小心“守护者”的大球，别让你的奶酪飞啦～！ ⚡ Watch out for the swinging ball — protect your cheese!
