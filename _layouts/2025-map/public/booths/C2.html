🎶 活动介绍 | Activity Overview

在老师的指导下，体验中国古琴的演奏之美。

学习基础指法与演奏姿势，

聆听古韵悠长的“高山流水”，

让心灵在指尖的流动中获得片刻宁静。🕊️

Under the guidance of an instructor, participants will explore the elegance of the Guqin,

the traditional seven-string zither.

Learn basic finger techniques and postures,

and experience the serene charm of “High Mountains and Flowing Water,”

finding peace and reflection within each gentle vibration. 🎼

💡 小贴士 | Tip: 静心聆听古音，让指尖轻触千年回响。
