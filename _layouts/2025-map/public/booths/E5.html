经典中的经典，老少咸宜的欢乐游戏！🔨💥 地鼠们需要时不时地“冒头”窥探、躲避， 猎人们则要用充气锤，在它们缩回前“地”地鼠！

A classic and hilarious family game! 🐹💥 1–2 Hunters stand in the middle with inflatable hammers, while 5 Moles hide around the edge of the circle.

The moles must pop up, peek, and dodge quickly — before the hunter can “whack” them!

⚡

🎮 游戏规则 | Game Rules

1️⃣ 圆形布中央为“地鼠猎人”区，周围 5 个小孔为“地鼠洞”。 2️⃣ 猎人手持充气锤，尝试击中冒头的地鼠。 3️⃣ 地鼠可随时探头或缩回，但若被击中即算出局。 4️⃣ 轮流进行，看看谁是最机灵的小地鼠！🐭✨

1️⃣ 1–2 Hunters stand in the center with inflatable hammers.

2️⃣ Five Moles play around the edge, peeking through small holes.

3️⃣ If a Mole gets hit, they’re out for the round.

4️⃣ Take turns and see who’s the quickest mole to survive! 🎉

👫 适合人数 | Recommended Players: 4人及以上 / 4+ players

💡 小贴士 | Tip: 动作快、反应快，小心别笑得停不下来！😂 💡 Be quick, be clever — and try not to laugh too hard!
