让节奏流动，用鼓声交流。🌍🎶 在这里，不需要任何音乐基础， 只需跟随领鼓老师的引导， 用双手拍打非洲鼓或打击乐器， 一起创造属于此刻的节奏与能量！🔥

Feel the rhythm, connect through the beat.

🪘✨ Join the Drum Circle and experience the joy of rhythm-making!

No music experience needed — just follow the facilitator, play your drum, and become part of a powerful, shared heartbeat.

💫

🎵 活动说明 | Activity Overview

1️⃣ 由领鼓老师带领，参与者围成圈加入节奏演奏。 2️⃣ 使用非洲鼓、手鼓或打击乐器共同演奏。 3️⃣ 通过节奏互动，感受团队协作与音乐律动的力量。

1️⃣ Led by a facilitator, participants form a circle and play together.

2️⃣ Use djembes, hand drums, or percussion instruments to create rhythm. 3️⃣ Feel the connection and joy of collective music-making.

🎶

💡 小贴士 | Tip: 放松身体，聆听节奏——音乐就在你的掌心！👐 💡 Tip: Relax, listen, and let the rhythm move through your hands!
